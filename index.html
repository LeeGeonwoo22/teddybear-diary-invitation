Why the bandage?

Cards are a metaphor for care.

In Teddy Bear's Diary, cards are not fortune-telling. They are a gentle way to look at a hurt, care for it, and help it heal.

🔮

Tarot · cleaning the wound

Tarot is like antiseptic and medicine. It helps you look at what hurts right now and tend to it with care.

😇

Angel cards · placing the bandage

An angel card is the bandage that protects the tender place from being hurt again.

Coming in a future release

Like the rainbow in the Ten of Cups: “It will not always hurt in the same way.”

— The feeling Teddy hopes to leave with you

Clean it with antiseptic (tarot), then help it heal beneath a bandage (angel cards). That is how the app treats cards—and why Teddy wears a small bandage too.

What you can do

A quiet place for everyday feelings.

01 · Chat

Talk with Teddy

Unpack the day in your own words. Teddy can notice rumination loops and suggest a small, manageable next step.

02 · Diary

Turn conversation into a diary

After enough conversation has gathered, the app can shape its flow into a diary entry for you to keep.

03 · Cards

Receive a message for the day

Reflect with a tarot card and its short message. Angel cards are planned for a future release.

04 · Settings

Make the space your own

Personalize diary length, mode, language, notifications, and other supported preferences.
