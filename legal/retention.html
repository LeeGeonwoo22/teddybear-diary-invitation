Data Retention Notice

Teddy remembers only what is needed.

  • Chat records14 days after creation
  • Card records30 days after creation
  • Diaries, emotions, comments, and Teddy lettersUntil account deletion or service closure
  • Account deletionIn the app or through the web request information below

Expired chats and card records are removed from the app and then deleted from server and device storage in turn. Server-side deletion may not occur immediately.

If you connect a Google account, supported data that remains within the applicable retention period may be restored after reinstalling the app or moving to a new device.

Some data may not be restorable if a legacy encryption key has been lost, the retention period has expired, or the data was deleted by the user.

When AI features are used, the content needed to provide the feature may be sent to the OpenAI or Anthropic API. The Service is not end-to-end encrypted.
