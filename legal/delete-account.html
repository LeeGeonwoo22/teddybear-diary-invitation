Delete Your Teddy Bear's Diary Account

You can delete your account and related data directly in the app.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open Teddy Bear's Diary.
  2. Go to the Settings (⚙️ Settings) screen from the bottom tab.
  3. Select Delete account near the bottom of the screen.
  4. Complete the two-step confirmation, including a re-login check when a Google account is connected.

If you cannot access the app

Request account deletion by email at glee98670@gmail.com.

Please include the Google email address connected to the app and a short statement requesting account deletion. We may request additional information to verify your identity.

Data deleted

After account deletion is completed, the following data directly managed by the Operator is deleted:

  • Account information
  • Diaries and emotion records
  • Comments and Teddy letters
  • Chat records
  • Card records

Temporary security, abuse-prevention logs, and backups held by external service providers may remain for a limited period under their policies. After deletion is completed, the related account and data generally cannot be restored.
