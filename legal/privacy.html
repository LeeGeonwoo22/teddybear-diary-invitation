Privacy Policy

Published: July [day], 2026 · Effective: July [day], 2026 · Version 1.0

Haruda Lee, an independent developer (“Operator,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), operates Teddy Bear's Diary (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Service.

1. Information We Process

The Service may process:

  • Account information: Firebase user ID, account creation time, and sign-in method
  • Google sign-in information: email address and display name
  • User content: chats, diaries, emotions, comments, card selections and readings, and Teddy letters
  • App settings: language, notifications, and diary-related preferences
  • Diagnostic information: app version, device and operating-system information, error codes, and crash data
  • Network information processed during use, such as IP address, for transmission, security, and abuse prevention

The Service does not ask for government identification numbers, financial account details, or precise location data. Please avoid entering unnecessary sensitive information about yourself or other people.

The current Service does not include an advertising SDK, and we do not sell personal data.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide sign-in and account management
  • Provide chat, diary, emotion, card, and letter features
  • Restore supported data for users who sign in with the same Google account
  • Diagnose errors and protect the Service
  • Provide notifications and user preferences

3. Service Providers

We use:

  • Google Firebase and Google Cloud for authentication, storage, server functions, and diagnostics
  • OpenAI API for diaries, Teddy letters, tarot and angel-card readings, and related generation
  • Anthropic API for Teddy chat responses

When an AI feature is used, the context needed for that feature may be sent to the relevant AI provider. As an operational rule, Google email addresses and display names are not included in AI prompts.

4. Retention

  • Account information: until account deletion or service closure
  • Diaries, emotions, comments, and Teddy letters: until account deletion or service closure
  • Chats: 14 days after creation
  • Card records: 30 days after creation
  • Diagnostic information: according to the retention policies of the relevant provider

Expired chats and card records are removed from the app and then deleted from server and device storage. Server-side deletion may not occur immediately.

5. Data Restoration

Users who connect a Google account may restore supported data that remains within its retention period after reinstalling the app or moving to a new device.

Some data may not be restorable if a legacy encryption key has been lost, the retention period has expired, the user deleted the data, or an external service is unavailable. Undecryptable content is not displayed as raw ciphertext.

6. Deletion and User Requests

Users can request account deletion in the app.

  • In-app path: the Delete account menu near the bottom of the Settings (⚙️ Settings) screen
  • Web deletion request: Delete Your Teddy Bear's Diary Account
  • Contact: glee98670@gmail.com

After account deletion is completed, account information and Service content directly controlled by the Operator are deleted. Temporary security, abuse-prevention logs, and backups held by service providers may remain for a limited period under their policies.

Depending on applicable law, users may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of their personal information. We may take reasonable steps to verify a request.

7. Security

We use measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS in transit
  • Encryption for cloud-stored content
  • Encrypted, account-separated local storage
  • Firebase Security Rules and App Check
  • Server-side storage of API secrets
  • A rule against logging diary or chat text, email addresses, or raw user IDs in error logs

The Service is not end-to-end encrypted. Content needed for an AI feature is decrypted for server processing and sent to the relevant AI provider.

8. AI Notice

AI-generated chats, diaries, letters, and card readings may be inaccurate or unsuitable for a user's circumstances.

The Service does not provide medical diagnosis, mental-health treatment, emergency services, legal or financial advice, or reliable predictions. For important decisions or emergencies, contact an appropriate professional or local emergency service.

9. Children and Minors

The Service is not primarily directed to children.

If we learn that personal information from a child who requires parental consent has been collected, we may restrict the relevant account and take steps to delete the information.

A parent or guardian may contact glee98670@gmail.com.
